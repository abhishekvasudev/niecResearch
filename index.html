About Conference

Northern India Engineering College is organizing National Conference on Advances in Computing and Communication (NCACC-2018). The conference aims to provide a platform to research scholars, academicians, scientists and mangers to meet and share their view on the latest developments and current technologies

About NIEC

Northern India Engineering College (NIEC), New Delhi was established by BBDES, LUCKNOW in the year 2003. NIEC has an aesthetically designed campus spread over approximately 9 acres of lush green environment at Shastri Park. The Campus comprises of five interconnected blocks covering a built up area of approx. 3.0 Lac square feet. NIEC offers Under Graduate and Post Graduate level full time Professional programs approved by AICTE in affiliation with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), Delhi.

About IT Department

Department of Information Technology came into existence in 2006 with the aim to provide quality education in the field of Information Technology. It works as the nodal center of managing computer resources and imparting training and function is to control and provide network connectivity and dealing with day to day network related issues. The department also has dedicated labs for Internet access and latest software. The faculty members have experience in almost all the specialization of Information Technology. Faculty members keep them updated by publishing papers in their domain in various national and international journals of repute. Few of the papers have been cited with Google Scholar, Scopus etc. Department always emphasizes on the quality of projects undertaken by students. Aiming at the quality project, department achieved second prize in GGSIPU project competition. Students carried their projects in Wireless Technology, Android Applications, Python, Routing, Packet Simulation etc. To keep students, faculty and staff well informed about the policies, notices etc, and students have developed official Android Application of NIEC, which is available on Google Play Store and securing huge hits.
